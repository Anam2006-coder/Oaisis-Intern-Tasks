What is a VPN?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) encrypts your internet traffic and routes it through a secure server. This hides your real IP address and keeps your browsing private.

Will SecureVPN slow down my internet?

All VPNs add a little overhead, but SecureVPN uses high-speed servers and modern protocols to keep your speeds as close as possible to your normal connection.

Do you store logs of my activity?

No. We operate with a strict no-logs policy. We do not record what you browse, download, or stream through the VPN.
